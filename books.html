My Book World

The Realm of Honestly Loving a Fictional World More Than Your Own

WARNING! DANGER! BOOK NERD INFORMATION AHEAD! (Just kidding, this isn't dangerous.)

Favorite Books: Fantasy and Dystopian with a Hint of Romance are my Favorites ;) People call me a book lover but that's actually an understatement. Big time. I love, live, breathe, and die books. I wish I could be a part of my favorite book worlds. I wish I could meet my favorite book characters. I catch myself wanting to be in a fantasy world. When I step out of the house, I wait a few seconds just to see whether some demigod or wizard or satyr will show up to take me to a magical world. When it rains, I wonder if Zeus is angry. When it's dark, I wonder if there are dementors and death eaters roaming around. I cry when characters die and I laugh when they are sassy (hem hem Persassy). I create book playlists and cry all over again when I remember the moments that made me sad. Percabeth in Tartarus. Ron leaving. Keefe thinking he's not important. Zoë dying. Tris dying. Silena and Beckendorf dying. These moments define me. So, no, I am not just a book lover. I would kill to be a part of a book, to experience the things my favorite characters did, and more than anything, I want to be with them. In my own fantasy.
